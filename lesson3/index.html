Site Description

The website for Narvacan Business Suite (narvacanbusinesssuite.com) describes the purpose and intended audience, usage scenarios, and other aspects such as its organization and structure, style elements, and so on. It will be used as a planning and elaboration tool for the personal site that will be created as part of the WDD230 course this semester.

Site Name

The website for Narvacan Business Suite will be narvacanbusinesssuite.com. As the name implies, it will be a repository of information about Narvacan local businesses.

Site Purpose

The main goal of the Narvacan Business Suite website is to educate users on what the Narvacan Business Suite is, the benefits of joining this chamber, and other relevant and valuable local business and entrepreneurship information in Narvacan.

This goal is met by providing a description of what a chamber of commerce is and isn't, an explanation of design principles, and the main tenets of the chamber of commerce paradigm.

Target Audience

The target audience of the website is described below.

  • Who Local business owners and entrepreneurs based in Narvacan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines
  • Age 18 to 65
  • Income Typically between Php 2 000.00 and above annually
  • Motivation Grow knowledge on chamber of commerce and Narvacan businesses in different industries, as well as improve competence and employability.
