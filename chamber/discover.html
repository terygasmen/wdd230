Demographics Facts about Narvacan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines

  • Narvacan is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Ilocos Sur, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 46, 234 people.

Celebration of Bagnet Festival at Narvacan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines

Held every December, Narvacan is banking on the towns culinary past to bring in more domestic and foreign tourist and reclaiming its position as the birthplace of bagnet, that delicacy that is a craze all over Ilocandia and to gourmands.

A Makeover of Mt. Carmel Church and Tower

Our lady of Mt. Carmel Pastoral Center is located at Narvacan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It is one of the popular Local Business listed under Catholic Church in Ilocos Sur.

Inauguration of the Narvacan Farmers Market with President Rodrigo Duterte

President Rodrigo Roa Duterte leads the inauguration of the first-ever Narvacan Farmers Market in the Municipality of Narvacan, Ilocos Sur on March 4, 2022.

Opening Celebration of Narvacan Farmers Market

As recognition for the efforts and valuable contributions of Filipino farmers towards the progress, welfare, and development of Narvacan, the local government unit (LGU) pushed for the establishment of this world-class public market to serve as an avenue for local farmers in showcasing and distributing their products and goods, as well as in attracting business development in the Municipality and its neighboring provinces.

Adventure Hub at NOAH

The Narvacan Outdoor Adventure Hub (NOAH), a very unique adventure camp and outdoor sports complex in Narvacan, Ilocos Sur founded in 2005, is another reason to visit Ilocos Sur (aside from the Spanish-era Earthquake Baroque churches and the Old World charm of Vigan).
